Growtopian's Prefix

Growtopian's default prefix is /, fortunately it's customizable with the /prefix command.

market

Search for the items from the marketplace, add items for sale and search from the list...
USAGE
/market <item name> Search specific item /market <page> Change pages /market sell {item name} * {price} each * {WORLD NAME} /market sell {item name} * {price} per * {WORLD NAME} Example of how to add new items to market, always put asteriks(*) /market remove <item name> Remove an item from your list /market mylist View your items at market
